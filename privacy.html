Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Trilocore ("we", "us") handles information in connection with our website (trilocore.com) and our security tooling, including the BEVM Scanner and Janus AI services. We aim to collect as little personal data as possible.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact information you provide voluntarily — for example, your email address when you contact us about partnerships, walkthroughs, or security reviews.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, collected through standard server logs to operate and secure the site.
  • Analysis inputs — contract addresses and on-chain data you submit to the scanner. These are public blockchain data; we do not require account credentials or private keys.

We never ask for private keys, seed phrases, or signing access, and you should never share them with any tool.

2. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and security tooling.
  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested services.
  • To monitor for abuse and protect the integrity and security of our systems.

3. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the platform (for example, hosting and infrastructure providers), bound by confidentiality obligations, or where required by law.

4. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described here or as required by law. Server logs are retained for a limited period for security and diagnostics.

5. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, email hello@trilocore.com.

6. Security

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your data and respond promptly to any incident.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@trilocore.com. For security matters, see our security.txt.
